DISCOVERY CALL PREPARATION CHECKLIST

1) BUSINESS CONTEXT
- One-sentence business summary
- Current offer/service list
- Current team structure

2) PERFORMANCE SNAPSHOT
- Last 3 months revenue trend
- Lead volume trend
- Main conversion rate

3) STRATEGIC PRIORITIES
- Top 3 outcomes for the next 90 days
- Biggest growth bottleneck
- Biggest execution bottleneck

4) SUPPORTING MATERIALS
- Existing strategy document (if any)
- KPI dashboard or spreadsheet
- Pipeline or sales report

5) DECISION READINESS
- What decisions need to be made after this call?
- Who is accountable for implementation?
- First action to execute within 48 hours
